How to Download Videos

Complete guide to downloading videos from any platform using VidSave Pro

Method 1

Using the Website (Most Universal)

Works on any device — phone, tablet, or computer. No installation needed.

1

Copy the Video URL

Go to the platform (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram, etc.) and find the video you want to download.

  • YouTube: Click "Share" → "Copy link" or copy the URL from your browser address bar
  • TikTok: Tap "Share" → "Copy link"
  • Instagram: Tap the three dots (•••) → "Copy link"
  • Facebook: Click the three dots → "Copy link"
2

Paste into VidSave Pro

Come back to vidsavepro.com and paste the link into the large input box at the top of the page. Click the green "Download" button.

3

Select Quality and Format

A list of available download options will appear. Choose your preferred quality:

Format Resolution Best For Typical Size
MP4 4K 3840×2160 Large screens, archiving ~1.2 GB/hr
MP4 1080p 1920×1080 Standard high quality ~420 MB/hr
MP4 720p 1280×720 Mobile, sharing ~195 MB/hr
MP4 480p 854×480 Smaller file size ~85 MB/hr
MP3 Audio Audio Only Music, podcasts ~56 MB/hr (128k)
4

Save to Your Device

Click your chosen format to start the download. The file will save to your device's default Downloads folder.

💻 Desktop

File saves to your Downloads folder. Right-click → "Save link as" for custom location.

📱 iPhone/iPad

Tap and hold the download button → "Download Linked File" to save to Files app.

🤖 Android

File saves to your Downloads folder. Move to Gallery with any file manager app.

Method 2

The "SS" YouTube Trick

The fastest way to download YouTube videos directly from your browser.

1

Open Any YouTube Video

Go to YouTube and find the video you want to download. Look at your browser's address bar.

2

Add "ss" Before youtube.com

In the address bar, click just before "youtube" and type ss. Press Enter.

youtube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ
↓ Type "ss" before "youtube"
ssyoutube.com/watch?v=dQw4w9WgXcQ
3

Download Your Video

VidSave Pro will automatically open with the video ready to download. Choose your quality and save!

Method 3

Browser Extension (Easiest)

Install the VidSave Helper extension for one-click downloading on any site.

1

Install the Extension

Available for Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari, and Opera. Click "Install Helper" in the navigation bar above.

🌐 Chrome 🦊 Firefox 🔵 Edge 🧭 Safari 🔴 Opera
2

Browse Any Video Site

Visit YouTube, TikTok, Facebook, Instagram, or any supported platform normally. The extension icon will appear when a video is detected.

3

Click the Download Button

Click the VidSave Pro download button that appears on video pages. Select your quality and the download begins instantly.
