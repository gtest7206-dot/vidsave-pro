Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Our Privacy Promise

VidSave Pro does not store the URLs you submit, does not track what you download, and does not require any personal information. Your privacy is paramount.

1. Information We Collect

VidSave Pro collects minimal information to provide and improve our service:

  • Usage data: Anonymous analytics (page views, browser type, country) to understand how our service is used. This data is aggregated and cannot identify you personally.
  • Technical data: IP addresses are processed temporarily to deliver the service and for rate limiting. We do not log or store IP addresses beyond the session.
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies for site functionality (language preference, session management). We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

2. What We DO NOT Collect

  • Video URLs you submit for download
  • Videos you download (we don't host files)
  • Personal identification information
  • Browsing history or behavior outside our site
  • Email addresses (unless you contact us)

3. How We Use Information

The anonymous usage data we collect is used exclusively to: improve site performance, understand which features are most useful, detect and prevent abuse, and maintain service reliability.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for analytics and infrastructure. These providers have their own privacy policies and process only anonymized data. We do not sell data to third parties.

5. Cookies

We use essential cookies for: language preferences, session management, and anti-abuse rate limiting. You can disable cookies in your browser, though some functionality may be affected.

6. Data Security

All data transmitted to and from VidSave Pro is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS. We follow industry best practices for security and regularly review our systems for vulnerabilities.

7. Children's Privacy

VidSave Pro is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data we hold about you. Since we collect minimal data, most requests can be fulfilled by simply clearing your browser cookies. Contact us for specific requests.

9. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries: privacy@vidsavepro.com
