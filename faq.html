Quality & Formats

Available qualities depend on the original video. For YouTube, we offer: 4K (2160p), 2K (1440p), 1080p Full HD, 720p HD, 480p, 360p, 240p, and 144p. For other platforms, quality options vary based on what was uploaded. We always show all available qualities.

Yes! If the original YouTube video was uploaded in 4K, VidSave Pro can download it in 4K Ultra HD (2160p). Note that 4K files are large (typically 1-3 GB per hour of video) and download speeds depend on your internet connection.

VidSave Pro supports MP3 (128kbps, 192kbps, 320kbps), M4A (AAC), OGG, and WEBM audio-only formats. For best audio quality, select MP3 320kbps.

4K is only available if the creator originally uploaded the video in 4K resolution. If the original upload was 1080p, the highest quality available will be 1080p. We cannot upscale videos.
